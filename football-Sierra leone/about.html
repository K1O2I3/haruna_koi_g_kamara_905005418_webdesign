Our History

The Sierra Leone Football Agency has been at the forefront of developing and promoting football excellence in Sierra Leone since its establishment. We are dedicated to nurturing local talent and showcasing the incredible skills of our Premier League teams and players.

Our journey began with a vision to create a platform that celebrates the passion and dedication of Sierra Leonean footballers. Today, we stand as a beacon of hope and opportunity for aspiring athletes across the nation.

Our Mission

To promote and develop football in Sierra Leone by providing a professional platform for players, teams, and fans. We strive to:

  • Develop local talent through quality training programs
  • Organize competitive matches and tournaments
  • Create opportunities for international exposure
  • Foster unity and sportsmanship across communities
  • Inspire the next generation of football stars
